About

Carol Baker-Lai

Carol is a contemporary artist based in Toronto. Her work is an exploration of the unapologetic feminine spirit, translated through a lens of electric color and rich, tactile texture.

By blending traditional figurative subjects with unconventional, neon-infused palettes, Carol creates pieces that radiate a sense of self-love and inspirational strength. Each brushstroke is intentional, building up layers that invite the viewer to not just see the art, but feel the energy behind its creation.
