About

Carol Baker-Lai

Carol is a contemporary artist based in Toronto. Her work is an exploration of the unapologetic feminine spirit, translated through a lens of electric colour and rich, tactile texture.

= By blending traditional figurative subjects with unconventional, neon-infused palettes, Carol creates pieces that radiate a sense of self-love and inspirational strength. Each brushstroke is intentional, building up layers that invite the viewer to not just see the art, but feel the energy behind its creation. Carol's mediums include acrylic paint, moulding and gel pastes, iridescent mediums, alcohol inks, spray paints, palettes knives, silicone oil, flow acrylics, sponges and other textured objects for prints and stenciled effects.
